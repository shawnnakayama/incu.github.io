About Our Team

INCU Consulting, LLC, is a consulting firm with over 20 years of experience. We cover a wide range of industries, from auditing services to valuation consulting.

Tsuneharu "Tony" Nakayama, CPA, ABV, CGMA, CIA, CFE, CAMS

Tony is the founder and principal member of INCU Consulting, LLC. He is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants and the Massachusetts Society of Certified Public Accountants. He has obtained the Accredited in Business Valuation (ABV) credential and Chartered Global Management Accountant (CGMA) issued by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants. He has been certified by the Institute of Internal Auditors to be a Certified Internal Auditor (CIA), and certified by the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners to be a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE). He is also a Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialist (CAMS).

While he was in EOS Accountants LLP assurance group, he performed various audits and review assignments. He also has more than 20 years corporate experience in the following industries: automobile and parts manufacturing, food processing, construction, insurance, trading companies, chemicals and pharmaceuticals, industrial machineries and other capital assets.

He graduated from Suffolk University in 2002 with a Master in Business Administration and a Master in Science in Accounting.

Li Xu, Ph.D, CMA, CAMS, CFE

With strong expertise in data analytics, Li excels at summarizing complex data to help clients find solutions to tax and financial questions. She has developed forecasting models that allow clients, both established and emerging business owners, to see their potential future financial statements and identify and change the driving factors to achieve profitable results.

Li has served as an ad hoc reviewer for many premier business journals such as Review of Quantitative Finance and Accounting, Journal of Business, Finance and Accounting, Journal of International Financial Management & Accounting, Journal of Empirical Legal Studies, Journal of Corporate Finance and Managerial Finance.

She has published in Journal of Business, Finance and Accounting, Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, Review of Financial Studies, Journal of Banking and Finance, and Accounting Educators Journal. She received her MS in Finance from Suffolk University and DBA in Accounting from Duke University. She is a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE), a Certified Management Accountant (CMA), and a Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialist (CAMS).
