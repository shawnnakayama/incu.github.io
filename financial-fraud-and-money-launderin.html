Financial Fraud & Money Laundering Detection

Our financial fraud and anti-money laundering (AML) services are built on specialized forensic expertise and deep institutional experience. We apply investigative and accounting skills to identify suspicious activity, assess control weaknesses, and deliver findings that stand up in court, audits, or regulatory reviews.

We support financial institutions, law firms, and corporations by conducting in-depth analysis of financial records, transactions, and business processes to uncover fraud schemes, money laundering patterns, or compliance violations.

Our team includes Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialists (CAMS) and Certified Fraud Examiners (CFE), with over 14 years of experience in AML testing, forensic reviews, and regulatory compliance consulting.
