Our Business Valuation Services

Preliminary Valuation

We determine the fair market value of target companies or specific intangible assets, helping clients prepare for transactions, audits, and strategic planning.

Purchase Price Allocation

Our team performs allocation of the purchase price across intangible assets for IRS, US GAAP, or IFRS compliance — ensuring clarity and accuracy for financial reporting.

Goodwill & Intangible Asset Impairment Testing

We conduct impairment testing in accordance with ASC 350 and 360 to ensure regulatory alignment and reduce audit risk.

409A Valuations

We help startups and private companies determine FMV of common stock for equity compensation, stock options, and investor negotiations.

Data Analytics & Forecast Support

Using regression analysis and other tools, we turn big data into clear financial forecasts and decision support for business planning.

Our valuation consultants hold the Accredited in Business Valuation (ABV) and Chartered Global Management Accountant (CGMA) credentials. With over 10 years of valuation experience, we bring technical precision and industry insight to every engagement.
