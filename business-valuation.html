Business Valuation Services

Preliminary Valuation

We provide fair market value estimates for businesses, equity interests, and intangible assets to support strategic decisions such as buy-sell negotiations, exit planning, restructuring, and investor presentations. Our work combines market research, financial analysis, and industry benchmarking to deliver valuation insights that are both accurate and defensible.

Purchase Price Allocation (PPA)

After a business acquisition, we help clients allocate the purchase price across tangible and intangible assets—including trademarks, technology, customer relationships, and goodwill—in accordance with IRS Section 1060, ASC 805, and IFRS 3. Our valuations support audit compliance and financial statement integrity by clearly documenting methodology and assumptions.

Goodwill & Intangible Asset Impairment Testing

We conduct Step 1 and Step 2 impairment testing for goodwill and long-lived assets in line with ASC 350 and ASC 360. Whether triggered by annual reviews or significant changes in operations, our testing helps identify whether carrying values exceed fair value, ensuring compliance while minimizing audit exposure.

409A Valuations

For private companies issuing equity compensation, we deliver independent 409A valuations to determine the fair market value of common stock. Our reports are designed to meet IRS safe harbor rules, support stock option pricing, and withstand scrutiny from auditors, investors, or regulators.

Data Analytics & Forecast Support

We use financial modeling, regression analysis, and other advanced tools to turn raw financial and operational data into clear projections and trend insights. Whether you're budgeting, scenario planning, or preparing investor materials, we help you back up your assumptions with credible, data-driven analysis.

Our valuation team includes professionals accredited with the ABV (Accredited in Business Valuation) and CGMA (Chartered Global Management Accountant) designations. With more than a decade of experience across industries—from tech startups to manufacturing—we deliver valuations that are precise, well-documented, and tailored to each client’s financial and regulatory environment.
