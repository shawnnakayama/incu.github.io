Advisory Services

DUE DILIGENCE

We conduct detailed research and financial due diligence to support mergers and acquisitions. Our work includes pre- and post-deal analysis of asset and liability valuation, with attention to areas like potential tax savings that can directly impact negotiations.

INTERNAL CONTROL EVALUATION

Through interviews with management and staff, site visits, and internal control testing, we evaluate how well your existing controls align with policies and procedures. Our goal is to help you identify strengths, close gaps, and ensure effective governance.

TAX ADVICE / TRANSFER PRICING

We review your transfer pricing documentation and help you identify strategies to build a more tax-efficient global structure. From compliance to long-term planning, we focus on improving both tax outcomes and cross-border cash flow.

Our lead consultant is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), licensed by both the American Institute of CPAs and the Massachusetts Society of CPAs, and is also a Certified Internal Auditor (CIA). With over 10 years of experience advising clients across industries, we combine deep technical knowledge with practical insights you can rely on.
