Advisory Services

DUE DILIGENCE

Conduct Mergers & Acquisitions research and financial due diligence. Perform the pre- and post-deal effects of asset and liability valuation, including items like potential tax savings that can influence the negotiation.

INTERNAL CONTROL EVALUATION

Evaluate the controls you have in place via interviews with management and staff, site visits and observations. Perform an internal control testing for the adherence to policies and procedures to gauge the effectiveness of the controls.

TAX ADVICE / TRANSFER PRICING

Assess transfer pricing documentation requirements. Assess strategic opportunities to determine a tax efficient global tax and cash flow structure.
