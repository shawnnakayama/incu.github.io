Accounting Services

Financial Statement Drafting

We prepare customized financial statements that align with your business operations and reporting needs. In addition to drafting, we offer guidance to help you interpret key financial metrics, empowering you to make informed decisions based on accurate and meaningful reports.

Bookkeeping & Period-End Closings

Our team handles day-to-day bookkeeping and manages monthly, quarterly, and annual closings to ensure your records are up-to-date and compliant. We focus on accuracy, consistency, and timeliness, supporting your internal teams without the overhead of a full-time accounting department.

Assurance & Financial Review Support

For clients requiring additional credibility in their financial reporting, we can assemble experienced audit and assurance professionals. Whether for internal stakeholders, lenders, or potential investors, our services help strengthen trust in your financial position through structured reviews and analysis.

Our lead consultant is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), licensed through both the American Institute of CPAs and the Massachusetts Society of CPAs, and holds the Certified Internal Auditor (CIA) credential from the Institute of Internal Auditors. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we bring technical expertise and reliable service to every client engagement.
