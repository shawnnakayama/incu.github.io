Welcome to INCU Consulting!

Here at INCU Consulting LLC, our business practice focuses on assisting Japanese and Chinese-related companies in the areas of business valuation, accounting, tax, and advisory services. Our boutique firm provides high level personal service and is part of an international network of industry experts. Whether it is for business M&A, fraud detection, tax reduction, or accounting service, we can help.
